Twenty Years of Browsers

The following browser thumbnails are from The Evolution of the Web, an interactive produced by Hyperakt and Vizzuality. The screenshots are ordered chronologically, starting with NCSA Mosaic 0.1 in 1993 and ending with Google Chrome 20 in 2012. I was not involved in the creation of the interactive; I merely thought it would be nice to compare all of the browsers simultaneously.


Source: The Evolution of the Web, Hyperakt, Vizzuality, Google Chrome.
